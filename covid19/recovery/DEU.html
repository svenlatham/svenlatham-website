Not more than two people. 2020-03-31 Jena first larger city to oblige wearing of masks. 2020-04-07 Launch of Corona-Datenspende (app) for voluntary use to monitor spread of virus. 50,000 installed the same day. 2020-04-20 (announced 15/4) Shops with retail space up to 800sq.m to reopen, + bookshops, bike stores and car dealerships. 2020-04-27 Face masks become compulsory on public transport (rollout date by state varies) 2020-04-27 RKI daily report estimates R Value at 1.0 2020-05-03 (annc 15/4) Restrictions on movements remain in place until at least this date. 2020-05-04 (annc 15/4) Schools to re-open gradually. Hair salons to open (under strict rules). 2020-08-31 (annc 15/4) No large cultural events before this date.
